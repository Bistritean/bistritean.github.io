DESIGN

Bring your own signed blueprints - made by an architectural office - for any modifications or 3D renders. We can also discuss drawing up a proprietary blueprint, which would then need to be approved by a professional architect or constructions engineer. By collaborating with other such architectural offices, together we can design the perfect blueprint for you.

RENDER

Once I have the blueprints, I can transform them into 3D renders according to your wishes and specifications. In this case, we can establish the level of detail and fidelity of the renders, from the environment and yard arrangements, to architectural details and even interior renders. According to your preferences, I can make 3D images and videos that present the objective from different angles and different perspectives.

BUILD

The 3D images and videos can be attached to dossiers for obtaining construction certificates and authorizations, for promoting a new real estate development project or for any other kinds of online listings.
