About me

Hi. My name is Plăian Horațiu-Gabriel and I am a Romanian student, currently studying for my master's degree. I have been making 3D renders for about 5 years now. I have been working as a freelancer since high school, and for more than a year I have been employed as Image Manager at H&P Housing Project, an architectural studio located in my hometown of Bistrița, Romania. I have not graduated construction school, but communications. Regardless, I am familiar with construction design programs such as AutoCAD, Revit and Sketchup, I have experience in this field of work and I am skilled with 3D rendering programs such as V-Ray, Lumion, 3dx Max or Blender. If this paragraph did not convince you, then I will leave the images in my portofolio to speak for themselves. 

FAQ

If you did not graduate technical school, then what did you graduate?

I graduated from the Faculty of Political, Administrative and Communication Sciences, majoring in Digital Media, in 2021 in the city of Cluj-Napoca.

If you are not a qualified professional, then why do you work in this field?

I started doing what I do around the middle of 11th grade, because I was spending the better part of my free time drawing up blueprints and 3D models. Even though I had not even graduated high school, there was not much in terms of competition when it came to this niche of the constructions field, so I managed to get a foothold in this regard early on. 5 years later, not only do I have a lot of experience with 3D rendering, I have also gained experience in this field.

How much does a 3D render cost?

That depends on a wide variety of factors, though prices start at $60 per project. In what file format should I bring the blueprints?

Any format you can and/or wish to bring. I can work with Revit (.rvt), raw 3D (.fbx or .cda), PDF (.pdf) files, images or even physical prints and boards. 

How long does it take you to make a 3D render?

Again, that depends on a variety of factors. Among those are factors such as the size of the project, what level of detail you wish, if any modifications occur to the building and to the render model and so on and so forth. With all this in mind, a regular house project with the standard image and video renders should not take longer than 3-4 working days.

Can I use the photo and video renders to promote my building?

Of course. Once I will send you the final renders, you may use them however you like.
